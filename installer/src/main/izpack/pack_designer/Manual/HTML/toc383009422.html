Redeploy a scenario


Topic content

The redeployment function is used to update scenario that are already in the system. It is recommended to stop the scenario before redeployment and reactivate it after the redeployment process was finished.

There are two ways to redeploy a scenario:

1.Using [Redeploy] button from the context menu available by right-clicking the dedicated scenario

2.open the details view by double clicking the scenario in order to press the [Redeploy] button

Figure 3‑18 Redeploy a Scenario – Option 

 

Like the deployment of a new scenario you have to select the appropriate .psc file.

 

Note: Redeployment will not change your landscape and user configuration back to designer settings. Your adjustments remain untouched.

ATTENTION! Exceptions are the timer. After redeployment, all disabled timers are back online and need to be turned off again.
